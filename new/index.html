  • Chi-TEK Tea Party The V&A Digital Design Weekend / London Design Festival 2011

    A curation of specially commissioned work, the Chi-TEK Tea Party (Computer-Human-Interaction) was a showcase and event hosted at The Victoria & Albert Museum beginning September 2011. Chi-TEK occured in London Design Festival 2011 and Digital Design Weekend, where the public was invited to interact with playful tea pots, created by women makers & technologists. I co-produced the project with the MzTEK team, developing the concept, writing a successful grant proposal, gaining sponsors, and designing & producing the outdoor event. Chi-TEK is supported by the National Lottery for Arts Council England, and produced by MzTEK, an arts project I co-founded in 2008.
